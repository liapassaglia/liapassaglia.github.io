about me.


Growing up I embraced my creativity through art and music so when it came time to decide my future career I knew one simple fact.
I needed a space to create.
When I took my first programming class, I quickly realized the world of technology would become that space. With every line of code I wrote, my eagerness to learn more heightened as I recognized the endless possibilites for design and development.

Today, I am currently a Junior at the University of Florida pursuing a Bachelor's degree in Computer Engineering and a minor in Business Administration. My main interests are in mobile applications, full-stack development, UX/UI design, frontend development and project management. This site serves as a way to showcase the progress I'm making towards gaining new experiences and skills and I'm excited to continue updating it with new projects very soon.
I invite you to explore the space!
